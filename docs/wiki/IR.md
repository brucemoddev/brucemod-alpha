# Infrared (IR) Module

Full control over the IR blaster and receiver.

## Features
- **TV-B-Gone**: Cycle through power codes for many TVs.
- **IR Receiver**: Capture and decode incoming IR signals.
- **Custom IR**: Load and send `.ir` files (Flipper format supported).
- **MakeHex Engine**: Built-in encoder for NEC, SIRC, RC5, RC6, Panasonic, Samsung, JVC, and RCA protocols.

## Shortcuts
- **ESC**: Stop transmission / Go back.
- **Enter**: Send selected code.
